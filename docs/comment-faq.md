# Comment FAQ

This FAQ is synthesized from visible comments in the private local research library. Raw comments, usernames and full author replies are not included in this public package.

## Common Questions

### How long should a lump sum take to invest?

Treat this as an allocation and execution problem: target allocation, cash reserve, rebalancing rhythm and emotional tolerance matter more than a universal timeline.

### Can QDII replace an overseas account?

QDII can be an access route, but it has tradeoffs such as quota, premiums/discounts, fees, settlement speed and product availability.

### How should VOO, QQQ, Nasdaq and S&P exposure be compared?

Compare exposure, concentration, volatility, account route and holding period. Avoid extrapolating recent high returns as a guaranteed long-term assumption.

### Should DCA/EDCA/VA change across bull and bear markets?

The captured stance is skeptical of over-optimization. A simpler plan that can be executed through drawdowns may beat a complex regime-switching plan that cannot be followed.

### Can leveraged ETFs be long-term holdings?

Before discussing return potential, explain volatility drag, path dependency, drawdown and liquidation/behavioral risk.

### What about CRS, tax and Hong Kong brokerages?

Summarize risk categories only: tax residency, reporting duties, broker policy, bank/card route and documentation. Users should verify current rules with professionals.
